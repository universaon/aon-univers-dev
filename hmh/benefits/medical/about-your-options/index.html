Make the Most of Your Benefits

Preventive Care Pays Off Getting regular check-ups and wellness exams can help you stay well and catch health problems early. It may even save your life! All of Meridianâ€™s health and dental plans cover 100% of preventive services when you use an Inner Circle or In-Network provider, so be sure to take advantage of this free benefit.
Consider a Generic Medication Meridian is taking a proactive approach to help you save money on your prescription copayment(s). For most classes of medications, there are generic medications which may often provide equal results. And when you use the Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy, your generic medications are at no cost. Your doctor is the only person who can decide if a change in medication is appropriate for you, based upon your other conditions and drug interactions.
Is Your Child Attending School Out of State? All services provided outside of the state of New Jersey should be scheduled with a Cigna OAP (Open Access Plan) provider. These services will be covered at the In- Network level. To locate an OAP provider, please visit http://sarhcpdir.cigna.com/mcoap. All other non-emergency out-of-state services that are not rendered by a Cigna OAP provider will be subject to the out-of-network benefits.
