Prescription Drug Coverage

Prescription Coverage Issue

Please be advised that as of January 1, 2017, we have experienced problems with filling prescriptions through Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy as well as retail pharmacies because of a technical issue involving eligibility for prescription coverage. There may be a delay in processing prescriptions through Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy and retail pharmacies, however we are expecting the issue to be resolved sometime later today (1/3/2017).

In the meantime, if you have a prescription filled at a retail pharmacy and pay out-of-pocket, you will be fully reimbursed for the cost of the prescription, less the applicable copay. Please visit www.envisionrx.com to complete a reimbursement form for submission to EnvisionRxOptions. You can also contact EnvisionRxOptions at 1-800-361-4542 for further assistance.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused and appreciate your patience!

When you enroll in any of the Legacy Meridian Health medical plans, you automatically receive prescription drug coverage. Prescription drug coverage is provided through EnvisionsRxOptions. You will receive a separate Prescription Benefit card.

The chart below provides an overview of how each plan covers prescription drugs.






Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy Saves You Money.

Copays are always lower when filling your prescriptions through the Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy. Contact the Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy for information on generic drugs and talk to your provider about whether a switch to a generic brand is right for you. You can reduce your out-of-pocket costs by using a generic drug whenever possible. Some specialty medications are also available through the Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy.


Specialty Pharmacy.

Specialty medications are very expensive drugs used to treat complex chronic conditions. EnvisionRxOptions has an exclusive Specialty Pharmacy vendor, Costco Specialty Services, and can provide individualized support, direct delivery, and clinically based care management programs to help you get the most benefit from the specialty medications your doctor has prescribed for you.


Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy Saves You Time and Money

Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy is known for its low copays, convenient drop off/pick up sites throughout Meridian and outstanding customer service.
All first time users of the Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy must register their demographic information prior to filling a prescription.

For JSUMC, Data Center, Parkway 100, Meridian At Home-Eatontown, MNR-Shrewsbury and MNR-Ocean Grove team members, you may register as a pharmacy user by simply picking up a form at your site or calling the JSUMC pharmacy location during regular hours of operation at ext. 67-64750.

For OMC, SOMC, MNR-Wall, Meridian At Home-Wall, Brick and Farmingdale, Ocean Care Center, Occupational Health-Lakewood and Toms River, Corporate Human Resources and Corporate team members, you may register as a pharmacy user by picking up a form at the Ambulatory Pharmacy located on the 1st floor next to the Gift Shop at OMC or by calling ext 680-4545 during regular business hours.

For RMC and BCH team members, you may register as a pharmacy user by picking up a form at the Ambulatory Pharmacy located on the 1st floor next to the chapel at RMC or by calling (732) 450-2602 during regular business hours.

All physician phone orders during or after business hours must be done through the JSUMC or OMC Ambulatory Pharmacies. If you have any questions regarding Meridian Ambulatory Pharmacy services, please call ext. 67-64750 at JSUMC or ext. 680-4545 at OMC.

Team members will have the ability to call in refills through the IVR phone system.

For Full Service Pharmacy and Off-Campus Satellite details, please visit the Ambulatory Pharmacy Website at My Wellness on www.TeamMeridian.com.


MERIDIAN LABORATORY SERVICES...

Offering convenience, accuracy, and quality – all that you want and need in a lab.

Inner Circle

Meridian Laboratory Services is pleased to announce our designation as the only “Inner Circle” laboratory provider for Legacy Meridian Health team members covered under the QualCare Inner Circle Plan, the QualCare CDHP, and the Meridian Value Plan. You can be assured that the laboratory at Meridian Health offers an extensive range of testing capabilities and has been servicing our medical community for many years.

To receive full Inner Circle coverage for laboratory services, and have your claims paid at 100%, you and your covered dependents must have your lab services performed by Meridian Laboratory Services.

Should you use a laboratory outside of Meridian, your benefits for laboratory services will be subject to a deductible and coinsurance. Services provided at a non-Meridian lab facility will not be covered at the Inner Circle level. Any lab work sent to Quest will be processed at the In-Network level and all others will be processed Out-of-Network. You will be responsible for payment of any bill you receive.

Specimens or blood samples taken in your provider’s office must be sent to Meridian Laboratory Services. Courier pickup is available for providers in Monmouth and Ocean Counties. Contact Customer Service at (732) 776-4138.

For a list of pharmacies and labs, visit My Wellness at www.TeamMeridian.com.
