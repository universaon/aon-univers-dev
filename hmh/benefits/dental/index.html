How Do the Dental Plans Work?


Horizon Gold (Horizon Dental Option)

If you enroll in the Horizon Gold Plan, you can choose to receive care within the Horizon Dental network or outside the network. If you receive care from an in-network dentist, your share of the cost may be less because the rate is lower than the rate charged by a non-network dentist. You also do not have to file claims when you receive care from an in-network dentist.


Horizon Silver (Horizon Dental preventive PPO Access)

If you enroll in the Horizon Silver Plan, you must receive care from a PPO Access network dentist or the plan will not pay benefits. Coverage is available for preventive care only.


Horizon Bronze (Horizon Dental Choice)

The Horizon Bronze Plan offers dental care from an established directory of Horizon (Dental Choice) dentists. The Horizon Bronze (Dental Choice) providers have agreed to a special discounted fee schedule, so your out-of-pocket expenses for dental care will also be lower.


Healthplex

Healthplex works much like a health maintenance organization (HMO), only its network consists of a limited number of carefully selected family dentists. You select a dental provider from the plan’s network who will be responsible for providing or arranging all of your dental care within the network. The plan will not pay benefits for dental care that is not authorized by your Healthplex dentist or outside of the Healthplex network.
