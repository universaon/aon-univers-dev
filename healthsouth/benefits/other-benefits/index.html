Purchasing Power

Purchasing Power is the premier program that makes it possible for full- and part-time employees to buy name brand computers, electronics and home appliances through payroll deduction. Purchasing Power is not a discount program—it’s a safe, responsible alternative to credit card or manufacturer financing. Cash purchasing is always less expensive than extended-payment purchasing. Purchasing Power lets you reserve your credit for other things, like emergencies. Unlike traditional financing, Purchasing Power sets a fixed price for its products and never adds finance or interest charges. That fixed price is divided into 26 equal payments, which will come out of your paycheck every pay period for one year—and then you’re done. Your product will be delivered within 1 - 3 weeks of placing your order. Payroll deduction payments are easy, convenient and safe.

Group Legal Plan

HealthSouth offers group legal services through MetLaw, provided by Hyatt Legal Plans, to full-time and part-time employees. The plan provides fully covered legal advice and assistance for most personal legal matters (employment/business related matters and divorce are excluded from coverage) for the employee, spouse/domestic partner and dependents.

Everyone needs legal guidance from time to time. From common legal matters such as wills, estate and financial matters, to defense of civil lawsuits, Hyatt Legal Plans provide comprehensive assistance. With a Hyatt Legal Plan, you can meet anytime with local plan attorneys to take care of legal matters.

Employees select from a nationwide network of attorneys or any attorney of their own choice. In-network, there are no claim forms required; attorney’s fees for covered services are fully covered by the plan. Out-of-network, you submit a claim form to Hyatt and are reimbursed according to a set fee schedule.

Hyatt’s Legal Plan is like having an attorney on retainer. There are no waiting periods, copays or hour limits, and there are no limits to the number of times per year you can use the plan.

This service is $7.88 per paycheck.


    Services Offered
  • General telephone advice and office consultations
  • Document review - any personal legal document
  • Wills and estate planning - living wills, powers of attorney, trusts, wills and codicils
  • Real estate matters – sale, purchase or refinancing of home, eviction defense, tenant negotiations
  • Debt matters - debt collection defense, personal bankruptcy
  • Identity theft defense
  • Consumer matters - consumer protection, small claims
  • Traffic ticket defense (no DUI), restoration of driving privilege
  • Juvenile court matters
  • Document preparation - deeds, notes, demand letters, affidavits, mortgages, immigration assistance
  • Family law - adoption, guardianship, prenuptial agreements, name changes
  • Defense of civil lawsuits

Identity Theft Protection — LifeLock

Identify theft protection is available to full-time and part-time employees. LifeLock is a leader in the industry and provides cutting-edge technology to protect your identity. LifeLock offers several plan options – allowing you to customize your membership services.


LifeLock Identity Theft Protection — Core Option

LifeLock Identify Theft Protection includes an alert if your personal information is detected on fraudulent applications for credit. Also included is assistance in canceling and replacing credit cards lost or stolen, removal of your name from preapproved credit card offers, monitoring known criminal websites for illegal trading of personal information, scanning national databases for new address information, direct access to domestic fraud resolution experts, 24-hour member support, and a $1 million total service guarantee (if you become the victim while you are a member due to a failure or defect in LifeLock services).


LifeLock Command Center — Upgrade Option

LifeLock Command Center option includes the core services mentioned above and provides data breach detection by monitoring peer-to-peer file sharing networks for files containing your personal information. Additional expanded monitoring of public and court records, payday loan records, and alias records is included.


LifeLock Ultimate Service – Upgrade Option

LifeLock Ultimate option provides the most comprehensive service, providing proactive identity theft protection and credit management tools. The Ultimate plan includes all of the features of LifeLock Command Center plus checking, savings, and credit card alerts every time a change to an account is detected; annual credit reports and monthly score; public records monitoring; and 24/7/365 priority access. The LifeLock Ultimate plan can detect new account frauds up to 60 days sooner than credit monitoring.

Step Up To Excellence Points Program

HealthSouth offers you more opportunities to be recognized and rewarded for exceeding performance and contributing to HealthSouth’s commitment to excellence within the healthcare industry. HealthSouth believes that employees who strengthen our reputation and goals for excellence deserve recognition. Some of these actions include: Patient Satisfaction, Training & Education, Wellness Participation, Safety, Community Involvement, Teamwork, and Quarterly Outstanding Employee Achievement Awards. Some examples of points that may be rewarded to employees for Stepping Up might include providing our patients with an excellent experience and a positive outcome; fostering an environment of working together; giving back to the community; or participating in the company’s Wellness programs.

Employees may let leadership know when co-workers go above and beyond by “Nominating a Colleague” on the HealthSouth.WeRecognize.com website. The website also allows employees to recognize fellow employees with non-points valued E-certificates to celebrate a birthday, wedding, new baby, or professional accomplishments.

Points earned may be redeemed for merchandise, travel, event tickets and activities. Visit the HealthSouth.WeRecognize.com website to view the Rewards Gallery and learn how to earn points through the programs currently in place. Any points you earn roll over from year to year, allowing you to accumulate total points towards items on your wish list.

The Internal Revenue Service considers employee recognition awards as income. As such, you will be taxed on the recognition points as they are issued and once awarded, the taxes will be reflected on your paystub after the reporting period.

If you terminate employment with HealthSouth, you will be able to redeem your points within 90 days of your last day of employment. If you have not redeemed your points by that date, a gift card for the equivalent amount of the points will be mailed to your home address.

Employee Service Recognition Awards Program

HealthSouth recognizes employees celebrating milestone anniversaries through the Service Recognition Awards Program. Beginning with the fifth year anniversary, and again every five years of service, employees receive a gift catalog from which to make a selection. The gift selections increase in value with every five-year milestone. The Service Recognition Awards Program also includes a retirement award.
