Frequently Asked Questions


QWhen will my coverage begin?

Coverage will be effective on the date of eligibility (hire date/change of status date). Changes to the initial enrollment can be made within the first 31 days of eligibility. The effective date for coverage changes made within the first 31 days of eligibility is the date the form is signed by the employee. We want to encourage you to make your elections as soon as possible.

QWhen will my coverage end?

Coverage will end on the date an employee terminates employment, the date an employee changes to a non-benefit eligible class or when an employee goes out on a leave of absence.

QWhat information do I need for my enrollment session?

Please have the following information available for your enrollment session:

  • Your full name, date of birth, and Social Security number
  • Full names, dates of birth, and Social Security numbers of each dependent you wish to enroll

QWho is eligible?

Visit the Eligibility page to see HealthSouth’s rules regarding eligibility.

QWhat are HealthSouth’s dependent documentation requirements?

HealthSouth requires you to provide documentation on each member you wish to cover in the medical and dental plans. Visit the Medical and Dental pages for details.

QHow do I complete my enrollment?

Self-Service Enrollment. Visit the PeopleSoft Self-Service Portal: https://psportal.healthsouth.com

  • Enter your HealthSouth Username/Password to login.
  • Click on the My Benefits tab.
  • Click the Benefits Enrollment tab.
  • Enter your HealthSouth Username/Password to login.
  • For each plan you wish to elect, click the edit button.
  • Click the dot for the plan type you wish to elect. If you are adding a dependent you will be prompted to add that information. Once you have decided on a plan type and entered all required information, click Update Elections.
  • You will then be taken to a new screen to review the elections you’ve made. If you are happy with your elections click the OK button. Click the Discard button for changes.
  • Once you have made your elections for each plan type and are satisfied with your choices click Print Enrollment Detail and print 2 copies. One for you and one for HR.
  • Click the Submit button.

QHow do I print a confirmation of my enrollment?

You can find your confirmation for enrollment by logging onto HealthSouth 360 >PeopleSoft Portal > My Benefits > Benefits Summary
