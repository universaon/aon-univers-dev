How To Enroll

OPEN ENROLLMENT IS NOVEMBER 7 – NOVEMBER 18, 2016

Open Enrollment is your opportunity to elect, change, or waive benefits for the 2017 plan year. We are excited to announce a new enrollment option this year, giving you the flexibility to enroll either online using our new self-service website or in person with a benefits counselor.

All eligible employees are strongly encouraged to participate to confirm their benefit elections for 2017.

Two Ways To Enroll

Online Enrollment: Visit the HealthSouth 360 Intranet home page and select the 2017 Annual Enrollment icon at the bottom of the page. The enrollment site is available 24/7 during the enrollment period.

Benefits Counselor Meeting: Let a professional benefits counselor help you choose the right benefits for you and your family. See your HR rep to schedule an appointment.


What You'll Need

Please be prepared for your enrollment session. You will need to provide or verify the following information:

  • Your full name, date of birth, and Social Security number
  • Full names, dates of birth and Social Security numbers of any dependents and beneficiaries
