Non-Tobacco Incentive

Non-Tobacco User Premium Incentive

In order to record your and/or your covered spouse’s tobacco status, you MUST complete an enrollment session either online or by phone. If you do not participate in the new hire enrollment process, you will automatically be considered a tobacco user and will pay a $10/week surcharge.

TEAM Members who certify that they are tobacco free will receive a $10 per week incentive on health plan premiums. If you certify that your spouse is also tobacco free, you will receive an additional $10 per week premium incentive for a total of $20 for TEAM Member and spouse. If you do not certify by completing an enrollment session, you will not qualify for the incentive.


Definition of Tobacco Use

“Tobacco use” is smoking tobacco in such forms as a cigarette, pipe or cigar; or smokeless tobacco such as snuff or chewing tobacco (including betel nut), within the last 90 days. “Tobacco use” also includes use of nicotine products, including electronic cigarettes and nicotine replacement therapy (gum, lozenges, patches) within the last 90 days.


Quit for Life Tobacco Cessation Program

If you and/or your spouse are tobacco users, you can still qualify for the $10 per week incentive by completing the Quit for Life program.


First: Enroll in the Quit for Life program within 30 days of your benefit effective date in the Tyson Group Health Plan

Second: Complete the Quit for Life program within 10 weeks of your enrollment in the program

Third: If you enroll timely, then within 2 months of your timely completion of the Quit for Life program, you must submit a completed Tobacco Certification form to your Tyson Benefits Coordinator to be eligible for the non-tobacco user premium incentive on a prospective basis and be refunded for the tobacco-user premiums you have already paid retroactive to your benefit effective date.

If the deadlines are not met, you will not qualify for the incentive.


Call Quit for Life at 866-QUIT-4-LIFE(866-784-8454) or go to www.quitnow.net/TysonFoods to enroll.
