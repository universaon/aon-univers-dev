Company-Paid Life, AD&D, and Disability Insurance


Several COMPANY-PAID income protection benefits are provided to eligible TEAM Members. You are automatically enrolled, and the coverage is provided at no cost to you! Please see your enrollment guide and your Summary of Coverage for more information about these plans.

Company-Paid Basic Life and Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance

The benefit amount for all eligible TEAM Members is: 1x your annual base salary (rounded to the nearest $1,000), with a minimum of $20,000 to a maximum of $1 million.

AD&D Insurance will pay an amount equal to the life benefit.

Company-Paid Short Term Disability Insurance

Hourly TEAM Members are eligible to receive 60% of weekly base pay for a maximum of 13 weeks based on years of service. Salaried and Management Non-Exempt TEAM Members are eligible to receive 100% salary continuation for a maximum of 13 weeks.


Company-Paid Long Term Disability Insurance for Non-Hourly TEAM Members

Salaried and Management Non-Exempt TEAM Members are eligible to receive 60% of base salary to a maximum of $15,000/month. Benefits begin after a 13 week elimination period.

Voluntary Life, AD&D, & Disability Insurance


Tyson also offers the opportunity to elect voluntary income protection benefits for additional security. The plans are designed to protect your finances, and secure a comfortable future by paying benefits directly to you, or to your beneficiaries. The plans are also portable, allowing you to keep the coverage if you end employment with Tyson Foods.

Voluntary Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance

TEAM Members have the opportunity to purchase additional Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance. The coverage will pay a benefit if you are seriously injured or lose your life in an accident.

Deductions for this coverage will be taken pre-tax, and benefit amounts are available from 1x to 15x your base annual earnings. Coverage is also available for eligible spouses and children. The maximum benefit amounts can be found in your Summary of Coverage.


Voluntary Long Term Disability Insurance for Hourly TEAM Members

Hourly TEAM Members can elect to receive 60% of their base pay, to a maximum of $10,000 per month. Benefits begin after a 13 week elimination period.

Hourly TEAM Members are automatically enrolled upon initial eligibility. Evidence of good health is required for late entrants.
