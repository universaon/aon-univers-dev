Documentation & Eligibility
Tobacco Use Certification
Enroll Now


You must complete the enrollment process, either online or by phone, by the 15th of the month prior to your benefit effective date to elect or waive benefits and to certify tobacco status for yourself and your covered spouse. If you do not participate, you will be automatically enrolled in TEAM Member-only coverage, and you will not qualify for the non-tobacco user incentive.

Enrollment Options


1. Online

You may log onto the online enrollment system at any time during your enrollment period to record your benefit elections.


2. By Phone

You may call 1-877-561-0240 (Monday - Friday, 8 a.m. - 7 p.m. CT) to enroll with a Univers Enrollment Counselor. Calling earlier in your enrollment window, rather than waiting until the last minute, reduces the chances of being placed on hold. Translators are available by request for TEAM Members who speak a language other than English.


What You Will Need

  • Your name, birth date, Personnel Number and last four digits of your Social Security number

  • Your mailing address and telephone number

  • If you are adding new dependents, you’ll need their names, birth dates, Social Security numbers and address if different than yours

  • If you are adding or changing your life insurance beneficiaries, you’ll need their names, addresses, birth dates and Social Security numbers


View the Enrollment Schedule


You MUST Participate in an Enrollment Session

If you do not participate, you will be auto-enrolled into certain coverages, and your family members will not be covered.

Coverage If you do not participate in an enrollment session...
Health Plan You will be auto-enrolled in coverage for TEAM Member only: Traditional PPO medical plan, core dental, core vision. You will be considered a tobacco user and pay the $10 per week surcharge.
Spouse and/or Child Coverage Family members will NOT be covered unless you elect coverage for them during your enrollment period.
Long Term Disability You will be auto-enrolled in the plan (payroll deductions will apply unless you waive).
Non-Tobacco User Premium Incentive You will pay the tobacco premium surcharge ($10/week).
Flexible Spending Account (Health Care or Dependent Care) or Health Savings Account No contributions will be made.
Voluntary Benefit Options You will miss the opportunity to enroll in these new plans without having to provide evidence of good health for certain elections.
