Medical/Prescription Insurance

The Your Perdue Benefits Medical Plan offers easy access to qualified medical providers through the Wellness Centers or at the office of a doctor. You can elect the Perdue Health Plan which offers care under the national CareFirst BCBS BluePreferred PPO Plan. The plan includes prescription drug coverage through Express Scripts, at no additional cost.

* Retail Drug Card: When you use a participating pharmacy, you will need to show your medical ID card and pay the applicable copay. There are no claim forms to file when you use a participating pharmacy. If you or your physician requests a brand name drug when a generic equivalent is available, you will pay the difference in the cost plus a $6 copay.

** Home Delivery Service: When available, generic drugs will be automatically dispensed, unless you or your doctor specifies otherwise. Send your prescriptions to Express Scripts, along with an order form and the applicable copay. Your prescription will be mailed to your home within two to three weeks.
