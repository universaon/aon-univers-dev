Wellness Center


If you work at a location with a Perdue Wellness Center, your Perdue Wellness Center can be your primary care provider for the Perdue Health Plan.

You can access the Perdue HealthWorks web site by clicking here.

When you use Your Perdue Wellness Center, you no longer have to drive to a medical clinic and wait hours to see a doctor. Instead all you have to do is call or step into a Perdue Wellness Center and make an appointment. Your Perdue Wellness Center offers the same quality care you would receive anywhere else – but you don’t have to leave work and don’t have to clock out. That means you are still getting paid while you’re at your appointment.

The Perdue nursing staff is licensed and ready to make sure you and your covered family members get the proper care. You can be assured that when you visit the Wellness Center, you’ll have the same privacy and confidentiality as visiting any other doctor’s office.

Wellness Centers are located on-site at most of Perdue’s operations and are open to Perdue associates and dependents who are covered by the Perdue Health Plan.

Whenever you see a doctor, you will get the most value from your medical plan by using the Wellness Center.




Health Improvement Plan (HIP)

Each Perdue associate has access to a HIP specialist, a medical professional who will counsel you, either in person at one of our 20 convenient, on-site Wellness Centers, Occupational Health offices or via email or phone if you do not live near a Wellness Center. A HIP specialist will help you create a personalized health plan to improve your health. If you have been thinking about losing weight, quitting smoking, eating healthier, controlling your blood sugar, or exercising more frequently, these are all the things a HIP specialist can help you with to achieve your goal….for free and during work hours.



For several years, this award-winning program has been available to our associates, and the overall health of Perdue associates has improved substantially, as shown in HIP results. The criteria that we measure for HIP includes:

  • Blood Pressure
  • Diabetes
  • Exercise
  • Weight
  • Tobacco Use
  • Cholesterol

If you have high blood pressure, high cholesterol, or diabetes, you are asked to follow up with a HIP specialist just twice a year to help you achieve your health goals. If your results warrant a visit with a primary care professional, your HIP specialists will make a free appointment for you at Your Perdue Wellness Center (if your location has one).

Because of the efforts of the HIP specialists, Wellness Center staff and most importantly, you – our associates – we will continue to get healthier each year. Additionally, associates who participate in HIP are less likely to spend money for out-of-pocket medical expenses. Almost nine out of 10 associates (90%) are currently in HIP, and we encourage all associates to participate in this free program to improve your health.


Important tips about HIP:

  • This is a voluntary program for your benefit.
  • HIP lab draws are testing for cholesterol and diabetes only.
  • Your personal health information (HIPAA) is not shared.
  • Enrollment process is only 10-15 minutes and you get a free incentive gift.

Please stop by and see your HIP specialists today to enroll in this award-winning program!
