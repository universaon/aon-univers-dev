The Perdue Savings Plan

Building a healthy financial future is just as important as taking care of your health needs today. Putting money aside for your future is easy with the Perdue Savings Plan. And with contributions deducted before federal taxes are calculated, it’s less painful to your take home pay than you might think.


Plan Features:
  • You can save up to 75% of your pay on a pre-tax or post-tax basis through convenient payroll deductions.
  • You can reduce the amount of your current taxable income by the amount you contribute (you don’t pay Federal, and in most cases, state income taxes on your contributions). And, any investment earnings are not taxable as long as they remain in the plan.
  • You can choose how to invest your contributions among the plan’s multiple investment options.

To find out more information and to name your beneficiaries, please contact Wells Fargo Retirement Plan Solutions at 800-728-3123 or www.wellsfargo.com/retirementplan.
