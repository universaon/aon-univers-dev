Group Accident Insurance

Compass Accident Insurance offered by Voya® Employee Benefits is issued and underwritten by ReliaStar Life Insurance Company, a member of the Voya family of companies. Voluntary accident insurance provides benefits for covered injuries and specified accident-related costs for an individual or family. Since health insurance only covers certain expenses (and plan limits can apply), this coverage is designed to help cover the out-of-pocket expenses that result from a covered accident.

Available only during the annual Fall enrollment each year


The plan covers a wide variety of injuries and accident-related costs, such as:

  • Hospitalization
  • Physical Therapy
  • Transportation
  • Lodging for family
  • Medical equipment

Plan Features

  • Benefits are paid for accidents that occur off the job.
  • You can also elect to cover your spouse and children.
  • There are no health questions or physical exams required.

Your enrollment representative can give you detailed information on the plan and costs during your enrollment session.

The plan has exclusions and limitations which may affect any benefits payable. Refer to the policy, certificate, and/or riders for a complete description of benefits, limitations and exclusions. Policy Form #: RL-ACC2-POL-12-NC, Certificate Form #: RL-ACC2-CERT-12-NC.
