Enrollment Instructions
Documentation Requirements
Tobacco Use Certification
Enroll Now

Tyson Foods requires Team Members to submit certain forms and documentation prior to completing enrollment. Please review the list below to see which documents you will need to provide. Click here to find the documentation cover sheet.

You may complete your enrollment before or after submitting documents, but any document change in the program will be displayed 2 days after receipt. Documents must be received no later than Nov. 11, 2016. *NO EXCEPTIONS*

Here's what to do:

1. Complete the required forms and gather your documentation.


2. Scan and email or fax these items to Univers or have your Tyson Benefits Counselor send them for you. Please allow 2 business days for Univers to process your documents.



  • Email address: Tyson.Docs@univers.biz
  • Secure fax: 610-362-8871

Documentation is required only for TEAM Members who wish to add new dependents or newly waive medical coverage or the spousal surcharge. Please refer to the email/fax cover sheet for details. Your Tyson Foods Benefits Counselor will provide the necessary forms for you to complete.


TEAM Member

If you are currently covered and want to waive the group health plan you will need to submit:

  • Current proof of other medical coverage*
*Other medical coverage must meet the minimum essential coverage requirements of the Affordable Care Act.

Spouse

If you want to add a spouse, you will need to submit:

  • Proof of current marital status

    • Copy of marriage license/certificate AND

      • A copy of the first page of your current year’s filed federal or state tax return (please blackout financial information) OR
      • If you cannot provide a current joint tax return, see your Tyson Benefits Counselor to determine other acceptable documentation such as proof of joint property/asset ownership (recent mortgage, credit card or bank statements, utility bills)

  • Proof of the spouse’s Social Security number or ITIN

If you are currently paying the spousal surcharge, or adding a spouse, and you are eligible to waive the surcharge in 2017, you will need to submit:

  • A Tyson Foods Group Health Verification form completed by your spouse’s employer, or
  • Verification on company letterhead that your spouse is not eligible for group health coverage, or
  • Spouse’s personnel number if covering a spouse employed by Tyson

Dependent Children

If you want to add a dependent child, you will need to submit:

  • Acceptable proof of the dependent relationship such as a birth certificate or adoption paperwork AND
  • Proof of the dependent’s Social Security number or ITIN
