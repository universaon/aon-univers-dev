Enrollment Options

Open Enrollment for Legacy Tyson TEAM Members has begun! Be sure to participate before November 11!

1. Enrollment Café

If there is an Enrollment Café at your location (this will be noted in your printed materials), please schedule a time for your enrollment. If you do not have the opportunity to attend an Enrollment Café session, you will have two options for completing your enrollment.


2. Online

You may log onto the online enrollment system at any time during the enrollment period to record your benefit elections for 2017.


3. By Phone

During Open Enrollment, you may call 1-855-720-9929 (Monday - Friday, 8 a.m. - 7 p.m. CT) to enroll with a Univers Enrollment Counselor. Telephone enrollment sessions are scheduled by state. Please click here to find your enrollment window. You are encouraged to call early in your enrollment window to avoid hold times. Translators are available by request for TEAM Members who speak a language other than English.

What You Will Need

  • Your name, birth date, Personnel Number and last 4 digits of your Social Security number

  • Your mailing address and telephone number

  • If you are adding new dependents, you’ll need their names, birth dates, Social Security numbers and address if different than yours

  • If you are adding or changing your life insurance beneficiaries, you’ll need their names, addresses, birth dates and Social Security numbers
