Legacy Tyson TEAM Members - Questions & Answers

You have most likely come to this site because you are curious about the new benefit choices
and enhanced coverage that will be available during Open Enrollment. Click on the
questions below for some of the answers you might be looking for, and explore this website
for more information!

When is Open Enrollment for 2017 Benefits?

For Legacy Tyson TEAM Members, Open Enrollment will be held from October 17 to November 11, 2016.

Will I have to participate in Open Enrollment?

Due to the updates that are being made this year, participation in Open Enrollment is highly encouraged. This time will be your only chance throughout the year to do the following:

  • Confirm, add, change, or remove benefits coverage, including tobacco-use status and the spousal surcharge
  • Make an updated Voluntary Term Life & AD&D Insurance election to keep your family covered (see questions below)
  • Enroll or re-enroll in a Flexible Spending Account (FSA)
  • Learn about new and enhanced benefits
  • Update or verify life insurance beneficiary information

Open Enrollment is your only opportunity throughout the year to make elections or changes (without a qualifying change in status event).

What new benefit choices, improvements, and updates can I expect to see?

We are excited to tell you!

New Benefits

  • Second Medical Plan Choice with contributions to a Health Savings Account
  • Hospital Indemnity Insurance
  • Commuter Program
  • Employee Assistance Program
  • Separate Voluntary Term Life Insurance Plans for TEAM Members, Spouses, and Children
  • Telemedicine
  • Group Legal Plan

Enhanced Benefits

  • Increased Long Term Disability for Hourly TEAM Members
  • Updated Basic Life & AD&D Insurance for Hourly TEAM Members
  • Increased Short Term Disability for Hourly TEAM Members

Updates

  • Family Voluntary Term Life Insurance will be replaced with greater coverage for TEAM Members, spouses and children
  • The spousal surcharge will increase from $35/week to $40/week

Why are these new choices available this year?

For all that you do as a TEAM Member, Tyson is constantly looking for ways to improve the benefits package that you receive. We’re excited to introduce these enhancements and additions as part of that process.

Will the benefits that I am currently enrolled in be changing?

Unless you cover your spouse or children under Family Voluntary Term Life Insurance, or are contributing to an FSA, your benefits will not change in 2017. Aside from Voluntary Term Life Insurance for Spouses and Voluntary Term Life Insurance for Children, the new choices and enhancements will be additions to your benefits package, not replacements.

What will be happening to Family Voluntary Term Life Insurance?

Those who currently cover a spouse or children under Family Voluntary Term Life Insurance must participate in Open Enrollment to keep their family members covered.

Currently, Family Voluntary Term Life Insurance includes coverage for TEAM Members, Spouses and Children in one plan. For 2017, the coverage will be divided into three separate plans: Voluntary Term Life Insurance for TEAM Members, Voluntary Term Life Insurance for Spouses, and Voluntary Term Life Insurance for Children.


Under the new plans, increased coverage options will be available!

I see that a second medical plan choice will be available. How will I know if this plan is a good fit for me or for my family?

If you are currently enrolled in medical coverage, your plan (the PPO) will still be available. A second medical plan choice with contributions to a Health Savings Account will also be offered in 2017. Because the new plan is built differently than the PPO, many tools are being provided to help you understand whether or not this new choice is best for you.

A short video on the medical page highlights the differences between the two plan choices. You can also view your enrollment guide, or speak with an enrollment counselor during Open Enrollment for help deciding whether or not the new choice is right for you.

Will the cost of my benefits be increasing?

We are excited to offer a new competitive and compelling 2017 Total Rewards package with even more flexibility for you and your family, and few rate increases! The company has agreed to accept an additional significant investment toward our benefits to keep costs affordable for TEAM Members. The rates for the group health plan will only increase slightly for Salaried TEAM Members. For Hourly TEAM Members, only the spousal surcharge will increase from $35/week to $40/week. In addition, many of your company paid benefits will be enhanced with even greater coverage.

Your 2017 rates can be found in your printed or emailed enrollment guide, and in the enrollment system during Open Enrollment. Materials will be distributed the week of October 10!

When and how will I be able to learn more about all of the benefits?

Details are available throughout this site and in your enrollment materials (including your enrollment guide). During Open Enrollment, you can speak with an enrollment counselor or participate in an enrollment session online for further information.
