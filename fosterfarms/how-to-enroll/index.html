How to Enroll:

The enrollment process is easy and we're providing resources to help you make the most informed decisions regarding your benefit plan. Please review the benefits information on this site to learn about the Foster Farms benefit plan offerings, then enroll using the instructions below:

Enrollment Checklist

  1. Prepare yourself by reviewing the materials included in your Benefit Packet, including this worksheet.
  2. If you are adding new dependents to benefits, provide a Social Security Number or INS Alien Number for them. Please also make sure you read the important notice in the "Dependent Eligibility" section below.
  3. Speak with a Benefits Counselor to make your enrollment elections whether you are enrolling in benefits or waiving coverage. Your counselor can help you make the right choices.

Monday through Friday, 7am to 5pm Pacific Time

Dependent Eligibility
Your eligible dependents may include:

  • Spouse or Domestic Partner.
  • Natural child, adopted child, step child or a child under legal guardianship of the participant up to age 26. Dependent children will be covered until the last day of the month in which they attain age 26
  • Unmarried, dependent child who became mentally or physically handicapped prior to their 26th birthday. Required documentation includes marriage, birth certificates, proof of joint tenancy, etc. Without these documents your dependents will NOT be enrolled.

Required documentation includes marriage, birth certificates, proof of joint tenancy, etc. Without these documents your dependents will NOT be enrolled.

If adding dependents, you must provide the supporting dependent documentation to Foster Farms Benefits or Human Resources Department by the end of the month following your benefit effective date.

In order to avoid a delay in providing enrollment information to benefit providers/vendors, it is best to complete your enrollment (this includes providing documentation to HR/Benefits) by the 2nd Friday of the month PRIOR to your effective date. If you were hired at the end of a month, you will have less time to enroll, therefore, though every effort will be made to enroll you timely, delayed processing of your enrollment may be unavoidable.
