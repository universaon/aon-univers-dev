Medical/Prescription Insurance

The Your Perdue Benefits Medical Plan offers easy access to qualified medical providers. You can elect the Perdue Health Plan which offers care under the CareFirst BCBS BluePreferred PPO Plan. The plan includes prescription drug coverage through Express Scripts, at no additional cost.
