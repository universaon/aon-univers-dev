How To Enroll

Open Enrollment: October 17 – October 28


Option 1: During Open Enrollment, you can enroll through the online self-service system at https://uenroll.net/CokeConsolidatedOE2016. You can access this site at any time of day before the enrollment period ends To log in, use the following instructions:


  • Click on “Enter Enrollment” at the bottom of the pre-login page
  • On the next screen, enter the following log-in instructions
    • Username: Employee’s Social Security Number
    • Password: Last 4 digits of Employee’s Social Security Number

Enroll Now

Option 2: If you would like to enroll over the phone, or have questions about the benefits during enrollment, you can call the enrollment center beginning October 17, 2016. 1-855-874-0282. You will be directly connected with a benefits counselor who can answer your questions and enroll you in the plans.
