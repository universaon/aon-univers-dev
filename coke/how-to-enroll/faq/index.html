FAQ


Q. How can I learn more about these benefits?

A. Eligible employees can speak with a benefits counselor at the Univers Enrollment Center, or visit https://uenroll.net/CokeConsolidatedOE16 during the enrollment for more information.

Q. Who can enroll in these benefits?

A. All full-time, non-union employees who work an average of 30 or more hours per week are eligible. Coverage options are also available to the spouses and dependent children of these employees.

Q. How do I enroll?

A. Employees can enroll by using the online self-service system, or by calling the Univers Enrollment Center at 1-855-874-0282 to enroll with a counselor. Please visit the "How to Enroll" page for further instructions.

Q. If I use the self-service website, how do I log in?

A. Click enroll, then enter the following information: Username: Employee’s Social Security Number Password: Last four digits of employee’s Social Security Number

Q. Can employees enroll in specified disease insurance or accident insurance at www.ccbccmyhr.com?

A. Employees can only enroll in these benefits through this website, or by calling the Univers Enrollment Center at 1-855-874-0282.

Q. After I enroll, where can I find my confirmation statement?

A. Confirmation statements for these two benefits can be found atwww.myUbenefits.com 24- 48 hours after you enroll.

Q. What is the deadline for enrollment?

A. Open Enrollment will end on October 28.
