The Advantages

In addition to providing great financial protection in the form of benefits that are payable directly to you, these plans offer excellent advantages.


Guaranteed Issue

Newly eligible employees will be able to enroll without answering medical questions. No medical exams or tests are required. Past the second year that the plans are offered, medical questions will be required when enrolling. Be sure to take advantage of this opportunity while you have the chance!


Wellness Benefits

Each plan offers a wellness benefit that covers certain health screenings! This benefit can pay $50 per calendar year per insured individual if a covered health screening test is performed. Examples of covered screenings are:

  • Blood test for triglycerides
  • Fasting blood glucose test
  • Stress test on a bicycle or treadmill
  • Colonoscopy
  • Mammography

Both the Accident and Critical Illness (Specified Disease) plans include the wellness benefit.


Portable coverage

Although these benefits are being offered through CCBCC, the plans are portable. Meaning, you can take the coverage with you if you leave the company or retire. You won’t have to answer new health questions, and you’ll be billed directly.


Affordable Premiums

The costs of these plans are conveniently deducted from your paycheck after tax. There's no need for you to directly submit payments.

The cost of critical illness insurance (specified disease insurance) will vary depending upon factors such as your age, tobacco use, and the dependent coverage you elect. Children are automatically covered for 25% of the employee critical illness benefit amount, at no additional cost. The cost for accident insurance will only vary due to the dependent coverage elected.

Premium amounts will be available when you participate in an enrollment session. You can also speak to a counselor about the costs at 1-855-874-0282, 9 am - 6 pm EDT Mon., Wed., & Fri., and 11 am - 8 pm EDT Tues. & Thurs.

Don't forget - since the plans provide benefits (including the wellness benefits) directly to you, you could even use the benefit amounts to help cover your premiums!
