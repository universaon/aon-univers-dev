Accident Insurance


Accident insurance can pay lump-sum benefits based on the off-the- job injuries employees experience and the treatment they need, including emergency-room care and related surgery. The benefit can help offset the out-of-pocket expenses that medical insurance does not pay, including deductibles and co-pays. Spouses and dependent children can be covered as well.

No health questions are required for enrollment!

Coke accident from Univers on Vimeo.

Examples of covered illnesses include:


  • Broken bones
  • Burns
  • Torn ligaments
  • Cuts
  • Ruptured disks
  • Concussion


This benefit also covers several services related to the specified injuries. These services include emergency room treatment, outpatient surgery facility fees, doctors office visits and physical therapy.
