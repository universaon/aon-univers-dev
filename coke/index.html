You will be offered two voluntary benefits that can be a great help when it comes to protecting yourself from the financial risks of serious illnesses or injuries! Specified disease insurance (critical illness insurance) and accident insurance can help to cover high out of pocket expenses that often result from unexpected events.

The benefits are payable directly to you, allowing you to use the money however you would like (household bills, deductibles, coinsurance, etc). These plans also include wellness benefits! Coverage is available for eligible employees, spouses and dependent children.
