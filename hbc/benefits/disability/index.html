DISABILITY INSURANCE

A disabling injury or illness, or even the birth of a child that keeps you out of work could have a devastating impact on your income, jeopardizing your ability to cover normal household expenses. With the right disability insurance, your income is protected, relieving you of the anxiety of depleting your savings to pay your bills.

SHORT TERM DISABILITY INSURANCE

Short Term Disability insurance replaces a portion of your income if an injury, illness or maternity forces you out of work for an extended period of time. HBC provides Short Term Disability coverage at no cost to you, and enrollment is automatic, provided you are eligible.

Who is Eligible: All full-time Associates working 30+ hours become eligible as follows:

  • Salaried Associates are eligible the first of the month following 60 days of service;
  • Full-Time Hourly Associates are eligible the first of the month following 6 months of service.

If eligible, you may receive pay for up to 26 weeks, depending on your approved medical necessity and prior medical disability leaves taken within the immediately preceding 12 months. Your pay while on an approved medical leave will be based on the following charts, however, prior leaves taken during the immediately preceding 12 months will reduce the pay weeks remaining.

LONG TERM DISABILITY INSURANCE

HBC also provides Long Term Disability (LTD) insurance to protect your finances when your disability continues beyond the period covered by the Short Term Disability plan.

Who is Eligible: All full-time Associates working 30+ hours become eligible as follows:

  • Salaried Associates are eligible the first of the month following 60 days of service;
  • Full-Time Hourly Associates are eligible the first of the month following 6 months of service.

Salaried Associates
HBC provides Salaried Associates with a core LTD benefit at NO COST to you. You can also enroll in the buy-up LTD plan, which pays an additional 20% of your monthly earnings. You pay the cost difference of the buy-up benefit through convenient payroll deductions. You also have the option to pay taxes on the company-paid LTD premium to allow a tax-free benefit in the event you become disabled.

Full-Time Hourly Associates
Hourly Associates can choose to enroll in the core or buy-up LTD coverage. You pay the cost of these benefits through convenient payroll deductions.

 

Plan

Benefit Amount

Core LTD

You will receive 40% of your monthly covered earnings, up to a maximum of $12,500 per month ($100 minimum or 10% of monthly benefit).

Buy-Up LTD

You will receive 60% of your monthly earnings, up to a maximum of $12,500 per month ($100 minimum or 10% of monthly benefit).

 

Long Term Disability Definition of Earnings

For the purpose of Long Term Disability premium calculations, the general definition of earnings for:

  • Salaried Associates is annual base pay
  • Full-time Hourly Associates is base hourly rate multiplied by 38.5 hours and multiplied again by 52 weeks (plus commissions, if applicable)

The premium calculations reflect the pay period immediately prior to or concurrent with September 1 each year. The premiums will be effective January 1 of each year.

Benefits will be paid based on earnings as of the date of claim.
