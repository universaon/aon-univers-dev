MDLive

On demand healthcare - from anywhere via video, phone or email - 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Prescriptions phoned in to your local pharmacy for pickup.
Only $38 per “visit”		 Cigna Member Associates:

1. Register online at www.mdlive.com/hbc.
2. If you don’t have internet access, call 1-888-726-3171 to speak with a health service specialist.
3. If you are enrolled in an HBC Cigna medical plan, you will need to provide your Cigna Customer identification number, which can be found on your Cigna medical card or by logging onto mycigna.com.
4. Complete your medical history so that MDLIVE can provide you with the best care.
5. Request a consultation.
