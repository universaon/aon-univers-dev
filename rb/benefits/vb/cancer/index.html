Cancer Insurance


Chances are, you or someone you know, have been affected by cancer. A cancer diagnosis is overwhelming enough to your mental health, but the financial impact can be additionally devastating. The cost for treatment and other medical expenses associated with cancer can quickly alter your financial well-being, particularly if your medical insurance doesnâ€™t provide coverage for cancer treatment. Cancer Insurance pays medical benefits directly to you, or someone you choose, to help cover the cost of cancer treatment.



Plan Features:
  • Benefits are paid in addition to any other insurance you may have.

  • You can also elect to cover your spouse, same sex civil union partner or grandfathered same sex domestic partner, and children.

  • The benefit is Guaranteed Issue, so no physical exams or blood tests are required to apply for coverage.

  • A cancer screening wellness rider is included, which pays $100 per calendar year per insured for specific tests performed to determine if cancer exists in the covered person.

  • Coverage is portable - you can take your policy with you if you change jobs or retire
