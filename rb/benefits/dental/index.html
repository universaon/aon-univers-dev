Dental Benefits


Good dental health is important to your overall well-being. At the same time, we all need different levels of dental treatment. It is for this reason that three dental options are being offered to eligible Raritan Bay Medical Center employees. Two options are provided by Delta Dental while a third option is provided by Healthplex.

The Delta Dental plan options outlined below are designed to allow you freedom of choice each time you need covered dental services, but the choices you make will affect the Plan’s reimbursements and your out-of-pocket costs. You can choose a participating dentist from Delta Dental’s directory of participating dentists, or you can choose a non-participating dentist. The use of a dentist within Delta Dental’s network (participating) provides greater discounts and no balance billing. Delta Dental provides coverage for preventive care, diagnostic care, restorative care, orthodontic care and other services.

The Healthplex dental plan provides services through Eastern Dental, a provider of dental care for International Healthcare Services, Inc., a licensed Dental Provider Organization in the state of New Jersey. Healthplex has no deductibles, no annual maximums and no claim forms. In exchange for minimal copay amounts, the plan covers preventive care, diagnostic care, restorative care, orthodontic care and much more.

The amount each benefit pays is different from plan to plan (see summary below), so it is important to examine your options carefully and choose the plan that best meets your needs.

CONSIDER THIS


Both Delta Dental Plans give you the freedom to receive care from a participating or non-participating dentist. The Delta Dental Premier plan offers a larger network of participating dentists than the Delta Dental Preferred Plan, while the Healthplex Dental Plan requires you to select a dentist that works out of an Eastern Dental facility.
