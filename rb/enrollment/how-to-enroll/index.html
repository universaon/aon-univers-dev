How To Enroll

You should have received information about your benefits and the enrollment process during Traditions, your new hire orientation. In addition, review this website carefully, as it will help you to understand the benefit options available to you and how to elect coverage.

As a newly-eligible team member, your health benefits are effective on the first of the month following your date of hire. You have 31 days from your benefits effective date to complete your enrollment. If you do not enroll by that date, you will not have benefits coverage in 2017, and your next opportunity to enroll will be during Benefits Open Enrollment in the fall.


Enrollment Options

  1. Team Member Self Service: Visit www.benefitsgo.com/HMH to access the online enrollment system. Follow the prompts to register and create your own login credentials. Sign in and click “Enroll Now” to begin. You will need the following information to verify your identity and set up your account:

    • Date of birth
    • Team Member ID
    • Home zip code

  2. Benefits Enrollment Call Center: If you do not have access to an on-site Benefits Counselor or the Self Service system, you can call the Benefits Enrollment Call Center to enroll with a Benefits Counselor over the phone.

    Monday – Friday, 9:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m. ET
