HBC Wellness Incentive


Living a healthy lifestyle is key to improving and maintaining good health. HBC is taking steps to encourage you to make healthy living a priority. The primary initiative to promote good health is the Wellness Incentive. You can earn incentive dollars in your HSA for 2017 when you complete your Health Assessment and free Biometric Screening.

What is the Wellness Incentive?

  • If you are enrolled in Associate Only coverage under the Gold or Silver medical plan options and you complete the wellness requirements, you are eligible to receive up to a $250* employer contribution into your HSA.

  • If you are enrolled in Associate + Spouse/Domestic Partner or Associate + Family coverage under the Gold or Silver medical plan options, then you AND your covered spouse/domestic partner must complete the wellness requirements to be eligible to receive up to a $500* employer contribution into your HSA. If only you complete the wellness requirements, you are eligible to receive up to a $250 employer contribution into your HSA. No contribution will be made if only your covered spouse/domestic partner completes the requirements.

  • If you are enrolled in Associate + Child(ren) coverage under the Gold or Silver medical plan options, only you have to complete the wellness requirements to earn up to the full $500* employer contribution into your HSA. Children are not eligible to complete the free biometric screening.

*The Wellness Incentive contributions are in addition to the automatic employer contributions you receive for enrolling in the Gold or Silver HBC medical plan and opening an HSA and may be pro-rated on a quarterly basis based on a date of eligibility or wellness requirement completion.


What are the Wellness Incentive Requirments?

To be eligible for the Wellness Incentive, you must complete the following requirements:

  • A confidential, online Cigna Health Assessment, (beginning 1/1/2017 - 11/30/2017)
  • AND
  • A Biometric Screening (beginning 1/15/2017 - 11/30/2017)

IMPORTANT: If you cover a spouse or domestic partner under an HBC medical plan, you and he/she must complete both requirements to be eligible for the full Wellness Incentive. If only you complete the requirements, you will be eligible for the Associate Only Wellness Incentive amount. If only your spouse/domestic partner completes the requirements, no incentive will be earned.

The Wellness Incentive HSA contributions are pro-rated based on the completion date of your wellness requirements. Deposits will be made as soon as administratively possible. You must be active and enrolled at the time of payment to receive the wellness incentive.

Next Steps

Take the Health Assessment

The online Health Assessment is a brief, confidential questionnaire that evaluates your current health and indicates if you are at risk for certain conditions. It also provides recommendations you may take toward better health.


Please note: To count towards the 2017 Wellness Incentive you must complete the Health Assessment on 1/1/17 or later.


Follow these steps to complete your Health Assessment:

  • Register and/or log onto your MyCigna account at www.MyCigna.com.
  • In the top left corner, click on the My Health tab.
  • Click on "Take My Health Assessment."
  • Complete the Health Assessment and view your results.

Cigna will automatically report your completion of the Health Assessment to HBC for purposes of the Wellness Incentive.
Please note: Your personal information will never be shared with HBC.


Complete a Biometric Screening

A biometric screening measures important health metrics, such as your blood pressure, Body Mass Index (BMI), and cholesterol levels. To do this, the screener will prick your finger to test a small amount of blood. These metrics, or numbers, provide insight into your well-being and identify potential health risks. They are a starting point on the path to better health. Depending on your location, you may have three possible options to complete your Biometric Screening:

  • On-site Biometric Screening at your work location (for Associates only).
  • Off-site Biometric Screening at a Quest Diagnostics Patient Service Center (PSC).
  • Complete a Physician Form following your annual wellness visit with your doctor.

Once your screening is completed, your results will automatically be uploaded to your personal online account in My.QuestForHealth.com, and Cigna will be notified of your completion for purposes of the Wellness Incentive. Please note: Your personal information will never be shared with HBC.

2. Free Off-Site Biometric Screening at Quest Patient Service Center (PSC)

You and your covered spouse/domestic partner (if applicable) can complete your screening at a Quest Diagnostics Patient Service Center (PSC) anytime between 1/15/2017 - 11/30/2017.

Please note, these appointments require a venipuncture (this is a true blood draw, not finger stick like the onsite screenings).

To make an appointment for an off-site screening, follow the steps below:

  • Go to My.QuestForHealth.com to Register.
  • Enter Registration Key: HBC2017.
  • Enter your Full Legal Name and Date of Birth as it appears in HBC’s system, including Jr or III, if applicable.
  • Select the event you want to attend and choose a time that works best for you.
  • Select ‘PSC’.
  • To select a nearby Quest PSC, use the zip code locator and find the closest Quest PSC to you as well as their contact information and address.
  • Schedule an appointment (some are even same day) or register as a walk-in and go at your convenience.

If you want to call in and get registered through the call support center, or have any questions about the scheduling process, please feel free to contact : Quest Diagnostic's Blueprint For Wellness Center at 1-855-623-9355 (Monday - Friday 7:00 am to 8:30 pm CST and Saturday 7:30 am - 4:00 pm CST).

3. Physician Form

You and your covered spouse/domestic partner (if applicable) can complete your screenings at your physician’s office. Screening results from a physician office visit dated from 10/1/2016 to 11/30/2017 will be accepted.

To complete your Biometric Screening using the Physician Form option, follow the steps below:

  • Go to My.QuestForHealth.com to Register.
  • Enter Registration Key: HBC2017.
  • Enter your Full Legal Name and Date of Birth as it appears in HBC’s system, including Jr or III, if applicable.
  • Select ‘Physician Form.’ For your security, each form is barcoded specifically to you and cannot be copied or duplicated for multiple users.
  • Print the form.
  • Schedule an appointment** with your doctor. The results must be received by Quest Diagnostics no later than 11/30/2017. Biometric Screening results from a physician office visit dated from 10/1/2016 to 11/30/2017 will be accepted.
  • Have your physician complete the form then:
    • Have your physician submit it by fax to Quest Diagnostics at the number provided on the form.
    • Or, you can upload the completed form yourself using the upload feature at My.QuestForHealth.com.
      You must have a valid email address in the system to receive notifications of upload success and form acceptance.

** When scheduling an appointment, it is important that you mention it is for your annual well visit. Annual well visits are considered preventive, and the cost of an in-network visit is covered in full by your medical plan.



If you want to call in and get registered through the call support center, or have any questions about the scheduling process, please feel free to contact : Quest Diagnostic's Blueprint For Wellness Center at 1-855-623-9355 (Monday - Friday 7:00 am to 8:30 pm CST and Saturday 7:30 am - 4:00 pm CST).
