MDLIVE


You know what it might be, and you know you need to treat it...now if only your doctor had a convenient appointment available.


MDLIVE gives you access to affordable, quality healthcare...anytime, anywhere.


  • On demand healthcare – never wait for a doctor again!
  • Access to US-based, board certified primary care doctors and pediatricians from anywhere via video, phone or email – 24 hours a day,
    7 days a week, 365 days a year
  • Physicians discuss symptoms, recommend treatment options, diagnose many common conditions, and prescribe medication
    when appropriate
  • Save money by avoiding unnecessary doctor’s office or ER visits
  • Prescriptions phoned in to your local pharmacy for pickup
  • HIPAA compliant and confidential; no individual Associate information is shared with HBC
  • Only $40 per “visit” (effective 1/1/2017)

MDLIVE Frequently Asked Question

How does MDLive save time and money?

MDLIVE offers a more convenient way of obtaining healthcare services – particularly when you can’t leave your home or office, or if you are traveling. In addition, it can save you money. The cost for each telephonic or video consultation is only $38, which is cheaper than the cost of most in-network office or clinic visits, and significantly less than the cost of an emergency room or urgent care visit. For Associates enrolled in an HBC medical plan through Cigna, the cost will apply towards your deductible, and/or may be subject to coinsurance. If you are not a Cigna member, the cost will be charged to a credit card.

Once you have completed your consultation, MDLIVE can even share any pertinent records with your medical provider upon your request and permission.

Who is eligible?

This benefit is available to all full-time and part-time Associates working 20 or more hours per week, as of the first of the month following 60 days of service, and you don’t need to participate in an HBC medical plan to use this service.

Your dependents are also eligible to use this service, even if they are not participating in the HBC medical plan, but they must be a dependent declared on your tax return.

Who should use MDLIVE?

  • If you’re considering the ER or urgent care for a non-emergency medical issue
  • When your primary care physician is not available or their office is closed
  • At home, traveling or at work
  • 24/7/365, even holidays!

Who can be treated?

  • Allergies
  • Asthma
  • Bronchitis
  • Cold and Flu
  • Ear Infections
  • Joint Aches and Pains
  • Sinus Problems
  • and More!

How To Get Started


Cigna Member Associates
  • Register online at www.MDLIVE.com/hbc
    • If you don’t have internet access, call 1-888-726-3171 to speak with a health service specialist.
    • If you are enrolled in an HBC Cigna medical plan, you will need to provide your Cigna Customer identification number, which can be found on your Cigna medical card or by logging on at myCigna.com.
  • Complete your medical history so that MDLIVE can provide you with the best care.
  • Request a consultation.

Non-Cigna Members Associates
  • Register online at www.MDLIVE.com/hbc-nonmed
    • If you don’t have internet access, call 1-800-657-6169 to speak with a health service specialist.
  • Complete your medical history so that MDLIVE can provide you with the best care.
  • Request a consultation.

MDLIVE Mobile

Doctor visits are easier than ever! With the new MDLIVE Mobile app, you can quickly and easily set up consultations and talk to a doctor from your smartphone. Users can download from Google play, Apple App Store or Windows App Store. Select the MDLIVE app and download to your phone or mobile device. With this free app, you’ll have access to advanced features, such as:


  • Provider Profiles
  • One-Touch MDLIVE Assist
  • Symptom Checker
  • Health History Form
  • Message Center
  • Online Payment System
