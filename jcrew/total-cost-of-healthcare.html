THE TOTAL COST OF HEALTH CARE

When considering the right medical plan for you and your family, it’s important to look at the total cost of health care. This “total  cost” is how much you pay in premiums (the contribution taken out of your earnings each pay period) and how much you pay out of pocket. The total cost also includes contributions to your Health Savings Account (if you enroll in the CCP).

The truth is that many people pay more than they need to for health care. By taking a little time to consider your choices and paying attention to your health, you could save money and possibly begin accumulating funds for future health care expenses.
