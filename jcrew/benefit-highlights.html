BENEFIT HIGHLIGHTS FOR 2017

We believe that our J.Crew, crewcuts and Madewell associates are our greatest resource. That’s why we take pride in offering comprehensive and affordable benefits to you and your eligible family members.

HIGHLIGHTS FOR 2017

  • Two Medical Options – You can choose from the Preferred Provider Organization Plan (PPO) or the Consumer Choice Plan (CCP).
  • Spousal Surcharge – If your spouse is eligible for medical coverage through his/her employer and you elect to cover him/her under a J.Crew medical plan, you will incur a $100 monthly spousal surcharge.
  • Elective Benefits – Critical Illness Insurance and Hospital Indemnity Insurance complement your medical plan, reducing the financial risk of dealing with a major condition. We’re also offering InfoArmor personal identity theft protection.
  • The Life Resources program administered by ComPsych provides confidential help with personal and work concerns.
