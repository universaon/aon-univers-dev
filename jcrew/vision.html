VISION PLAN

The Vision Plan covers eyeglass lenses and contacts every year. Plus, you can get new frames every two years.

You may use in-network or out-of-network providers, but you pay less when you use an in-network VSP provider. Review the chart below to see how you can save money and maximize your vision benefits when you use VSP providers. Visit www.vsp.com to find a network provider.

CHOOSING A VISION CARE PROVIDER

You can see a VSP provider or retail chain affiliate. Visit vsp.com to find participants.
