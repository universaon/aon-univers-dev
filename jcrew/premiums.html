2017 PREMIUMS

*SPOUSAL SURCHARGE

If your spouse is eligible for medical coverage through his/her employer and you elect to cover him/her under a J.Crew medical plan, you will incur a $100 monthly spousal surcharge. An associate who intentionally falsifies his/her spouse as not having access to coverage through an employer will be immediately subject to the spousal surcharge and may be subjected to termination of the health plan with responsibility for all claims incurred, as well as discipline up to and including termination of employment.
