Eligible employees have within 30 days to enroll from their date of hire.

Enrolling for voluntary benefits is easy. Simply follow these steps.

  • Have your personal, dependent and beneficiary information ready. You will need full names, dates of birth and Social Security Numbers of your dependent(s).
  • Speak with a Benefits Counselor. Your Benefits Counselor can explain your coverage options, answer your questions and record your enrollment elections.

Option 1: During Enrollment, you can enroll through the online self-service system by clicking thie link below, then enter the following information.


  • Username: Your Social Security Number or Employee ID
  • Password: The last four digits of your Social Security Number followed by your 2 digit birth month and your 2 digit birth year. For example: If the last four of your SSN is 1234 and your DOB is 07/01/2016, then your Password is, 12340716.

Option 2: If you would like to enroll over the phone, or have questions about the benefits while enrolling, you can call the enrollment center. You will be directly connected with a benefits counselor who can answer your questions and enroll you in the plans. 1-888-242-8760, MONDAY - FRIDAY, 8AM TO 5PM PST.


Confirmation Statement

After 24 hours of completing your enrollment, you can go online to review and print your Benefits Confirmation Statement. It is your responsibility to review the statement, and contact the enrollment center with any changes. Please log on to www.myUBenefits.com and follow the directions for first time users. This statement will be available to view for the entire calendar year, but it is your responsibility to review the statement, and contact the enrollment center with any changes.
