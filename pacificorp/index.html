No one expects to suffer from a serious illness or accident, but the financial consequences can be a real challenge. Treating a major illness or injury normally means that out-of-pocket expenses can be quite high, especially if you have a high deductible health plan.

If you’re stricken with a major illness or injury, do you have enough to cover your medical plan’s deductible and coinsurance?

There are plenty of other expenses you might not even think about until it happens (for instance, the cost for your family to stay near the hospital or the lost earnings from being out of work).

Voluntary benefits enhance your medical coverage, offering additional protection against the cost of unexpected medical costs.
