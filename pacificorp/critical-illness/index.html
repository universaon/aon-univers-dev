Critical Illness

A serious illness can strike at any time. Aside from the usual anxiety, you have to deal with the financial impact as well. The fees for doctors, tests, hospital stays, and other services add up quickly, leaving many people to rely on their savings and credit cards.

Critical Illness Insurance helps provide financial protection in the event of a covered serious illness.

The way it works is simple. The policy pays the benefit directly to you if you are diagnosed with a covered condition. You can use this benefit any way you choose – to pay deductibles and coinsurance, to cover expenses your family incurs to be by your side, or simply to replace your lost earnings from being out of work. The benefit amount is $10,000. You are paid this amount once you are diagnosed.

COVERED ILLNESSES INCLUDE:

  • Heart Attack
  • Stroke
  • Complete Blindness
  • Coma
  • Paralysis
  • Invasive Cancer
  • Carcinoma in Situ*
  • Major Organ Transplant
  • End Stage Renal Failure
  • Advanced Parkinson’s*

*You receive 25% of the full benefit for these conditions.


PLAN FEATURES:

  • You can purchase coverage for your spouse.
  • Covered dependents receive 50% of your benefit amount.
  • Benefits are paid regardless of any other coverage.
  • Your Critical Illness policy includes a wellness benefit that pays $50 once per year.
  • Coverage is portable — you can take your policy with you if you change jobs or retire.
