Voluntary Critical Illness Insurance


Unum’s Group Critical Illness Insurance can help protect your finances from the expense of a serious health problem, such as a stroke or heart attack. You choose a lump-sum benefit up to $30,000 that’s paid directly to you at the first diagnosis of a covered condition. You can use the benefit any way you choose. Each condition is payable once per lifetime, per covered individual. Benefits are payable for remaining covered conditions if the diagnoses are separated by at least 90 days and deemed medically unrelated.


 

Examples of Covered Illnesses Include:

  • Heart Attack
  • Stroke
  • Major Organ Transplant
  • End Stage Renal (Kidney) Failure
  • Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery*
  • Cancer and Carcinoma in Situ*
    (coverage maybe available as an optional rider)
  • Benign brain tumor
  • Blindness
  • Coma resulting from traumatic brain injury
  • Permanent paralysis due to a covered accident
  • Additional covered conditions for children:
    • Down Syndrome
    • Cerebral Palsy
    • Cystic Fibrosis
    • Cleft lip or palate
    • Spina Bifida

*The coverage pays 25% of the face amount of the policy once per lifetime for coronary bypass surgery and carcinoma in situ.


Plan Features:

  • You do not have to be terminally ill to receive benefits.
  • Coverage options are available for spouse and children as riders to your coverage.
  • A Wellness Benefit is included, which can pay $50 per calendar year per insured individual if a covered health screening test is performed, including blood tests, chest X-rays, stress tests, mammograms and colonoscopies.

Newly Eligible TEAM Members can enroll in Critical Illness Insurance up to guaranteed-issue limits with no Evidence of Insurability required.

Non-hourly TEAM Members:
Your biometric screening qualifies you for the $50 Wellness benefit.
