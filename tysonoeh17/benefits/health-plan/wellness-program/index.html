New Wellness Program


The biometric screening and wellness rewards program through RedBrick will end December 31st. If you are currently enrolled in the Hillshire Brands Health Select 70/80 Medical Plans, any rewards you earn through December 31st will continue to be added to your Health Care Account. See the Hillshire Brands Accounts page for details about how to access these funds after January 1, 2017.

The existing Hillshire Brands health club subsidy reimbursement program will no longer be available beginning January 1, 2017. You can submit a reimbursement form to payroll for eligible 2016 dues no later than January 31, 2017. Discounts to more than 10,000 fitness facilities nationwide are available on the Tyson TEAM Member discount site through Beneplace at www.beneplace.com/tyson.

For Management, Management Support and Technical Support TEAM Members

Beginning January 1, 2017, all Management, Management Support and Technical Support TEAM Members enrolled in the Group Health Plan will be eligible to participate in the Tyson Foods Wellness Program. The program is designed to drive personal accountability for your health and lifestyle. Screenings are conducted once each fall and results are used to determine additional healthcare premium deductions the following calendar year if goals are not met. If TEAM Members and/or covered spouses choose not to participate, the maximum surcharge of $21.54 per week per person will be payroll deducted beginning January 1 of the next year. Support programs are available to participants at no cost who are subject to the screening. All TEAM Members who are eligible to participate in the Wellness Program will receive additional details at a later date.

For Hourly TEAM Members

Hourly production TEAM Members are encouraged to visit their primary care physician to take advantage of the annual physical and preventive screening services available at no cost through the Group Health Plan.
