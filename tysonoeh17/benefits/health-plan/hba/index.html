Hillshire Brands Health Care Account
& 2016 Flexible Spending Account Funds


Although no additional contributions can be made to your existing Hillshire Brands Health Care Account (HCA) or to your 2016 Hillshire Brands Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs) after 2016, you can still access the funds that are already in your accounts to pay for qualified health care expenses. Existing funds in your Hillshire Brands Health Care Account (HCA) will be deposited into a pre-tax Health Reimbursement Arrangement (HRA) through WageWorks.

Your 2017 benefit elections will determine the order in which your funds can be used. Please check your balance(s) before you enroll.

If you elect the Traditional PPO Plan with a Health Care Flexible Spending Account…

If you have 2016 funds remaining, make sure you exhaust these funds before you use any 2017 Health Care FSA funds through WageWorks, but no later than 3/15/17. 		Any existing funds must be used 3rd, after any 2016 and 2017 Health Care FSA funds have been exhausted.

If you elect the High Deductible Health Plan with contributions to a Health Savings Account…

2016 Hillshire Brands Health Care
Flexible Spending Account 		Hillshire Brands Health Reimbursement Arrangement 2017 Tyson Foods Contributions to a
Health Savings Account
In order for you to receive or make contributions to your HSA starting in January 2017, you must have a $0 balance in your 2016 Health Care FSA before January 1, 2017.

If you do not exhaust the funds in your 2016 Health Care FSA before January 1, 2017, contributions from Tyson and/or your paycheck to the HSA will not begin until 4/1/17.

If you still have 2016 funds remaining on/after January 1, 2017, make sure you exhaust these funds before 3/15/17 (the end of the grace period). You must use these funds 1st for qualified health care expenses. Contributions to your HSA can begin 4/1/17. 		These funds must be used 2nd, after any 2016 Health Care FSA funds have been exhausted. When paired with an HSA, funds are limited to dental and vision expenses until the HDHP deductible has been met. After you meet the deductible, funds can be used for all IRS eligible expenses, including medical and prescription drugs. If you have funds in your 2016 Health Care FSA on January 1, 2017, contributions from Tyson and/or your paycheck to the HSA will not begin until 4/1/17. These funds must be used 3rd, after any 2016 Health Care FSA funds and any available funds in your 2017 HRA have been exhausted.
