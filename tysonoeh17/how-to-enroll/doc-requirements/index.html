Enroll Now

Tyson Foods requires Team Members to submit certain forms and documentation prior to completing enrollment. Please review the list below to see which documents you will need to provide. Click here to find the documentation cover sheet.

You may complete your enrollment before or after submitting documents, but any document change in the program will be displayed 2 days after receipt. Documents must be received no later than Nov. 11, 2016. *NO EXCEPTIONS*

Here's what to do:

1. Complete the required forms and gather your documentation.


2. Scan and email or fax these items to Univers. Please allow 2 business days for Univers to process your documents.



  • Email address: Tyson.Docs@univers.biz
  • Secure fax: 610-362-8871

At this time, documentation is required only for TEAM Members who wish to waive medical coverage, waive long term disability coverage, or add eligible family members that are not currently listed in the enrollment system when you enroll. Please refer to the email/fax cover sheet for details. The necessary forms for you to complete can be found on the Important Documents page.

If you currently cover eligible dependents under your Hillshire Brands benefits, and they are listed in the enrollment system during Open Enrollment, you will not have to submit documentation for them until the dependent eligibility review in 2017. Please see below for more information about the review.


TEAM Member

If you want to waive the group health plan, you will need to submit:

  • Current proof of other medical coverage*
*Other medical coverage must meet the minimum essential coverage requirements of the Affordable Care Act.

If you want to waive long term disability coverage, you will need to submit:

  • A signed waiver of coverage form.

Spouse

If you want to cover a spouse who is not listed in the enrollment system during Open Enrollment, you will need to submit:

  • Proof of current marital status (if married for one year or more):

    • Copy of marriage license/certificate AND

      • A copy of the first page of your current year’s filed federal or state tax return (please blackout financial information) OR
      • If you cannot provide a current joint tax return, contact the Tyson Benefits Center to determine other acceptable documentation such as proof of joint property/asset ownership (recent mortgage, credit card or bank statements, utility bills)

  • Proof of the spouse’s Social Security number or ITIN

Dependent Children

If you want to cover a dependent child who is not listed in the enrollment system during Open Enrollment, you will need to submit:

  • Acceptable proof of the dependent relationship such as a birth certificate or adoption paperwork AND
  • Proof of the dependent’s Social Security number or ITIN
