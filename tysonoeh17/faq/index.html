Legacy Hillshire TEAM Members - Questions & Answers

All TEAM Members will enroll in a new Tyson Foods benefits package for 2017. We are excited to share many benefit offerings that are new to Legacy Hillshire TEAM Members!

You have most likely come to this site because you are curious about the full selection of benefits, and new choices that will be available during Open Enrollment. Click on the questions below for some of the answers you might be looking for, and explore this website for more information!

Will my benefits be changing in 2017?

Yes. Your current Hillshire Brands coverage will continue until the end of the year, and you must elect new plans under the Tyson Foods Benefits Package during Open Enrollment. You will have a robust selection of benefits to choose from.

Will I be able to keep any of my current benefits in 2017?

Other than the Hyatt Legal Plan, the existing Hillshire Brands benefits will end December 31, 2016. The Hyatt Legal Plan coverage will roll over, and will be offered at a lower rate due to plan changes. If you would like to opt-out of the coverage, you will have the option during Open Enrollment.

A Commuter and Flexible Spending Accounts Program will be offered, but administered by different vendors and with additional program features. You will need to participate in Open Enrollment to enroll in these benefits. Your Basic Life Insurance coverage of one times your annual base salary will continue to be company-paid, and you will be offered the same Voluntary Life Insurance benefit amounts as your existing coverage. You will also have the opportunity to increase your Voluntary Life Insurance coverage this year during Open Enrollment.

When is Open Enrollment for 2017 Benefits?

Open Enrollment for Legacy Hillshire Brands TEAM Members will be held October 31 – November 11, 2016.

Will I have to participate in Open Enrollment?

Yes. In order to select new coverage options, provide coverage for your dependents, or to waive coverage, you must participate in Open Enrollment. Otherwise, your current coverage will end, and you will be automatically enrolled into default coverage that might not be the best fit for you! Dependents will not be covered unless you elect coverage for them during the enrollment period. Open Enrollment is your only opportunity throughout the year to make elections or changes (without a Qualifying Life Event).

How will I be able to enroll?

You will have the opportunity to enroll in your benefits with the help of an experienced enrollment counselor. The counselor will be able to answer questions you might have about the new coverage. You will also have the option to enroll via the online enrollment system that can be accessed through this site.

    • What is a Health Savings Account?

    Depending on which medical plan you elect during enrollment, you will have access to either a Health Care Flexible Spending Account (previously available through Hillshire Brands) or a Health Savings Account.

    The HSA is an account that is used to pay for qualified health care expenses and has an impressive tax advantage. Tyson Foods will make a financial contribution to your HSA, and you will also be able to contribute funds on a pre-tax basis. If you don’t spend all of the money in 2017, all available funds roll over year after year, and allow the account to grow over time. The account is yours to keep – even if you leave Tyson Foods. our company paid benefits will be enhanced with even greater coverage.

    If I currently have funds in my Hillshire Brands Health Care Account, will I be able to access them in 2017?

    Yes. Any funds existing in your HCA on December 31, 2016, will be deposited into a pre-tax Health Reimbursement Arrangement through WageWorks. You will have up to 5 years to use these funds to pay for qualified health care expenses (medical, dental, vision, and prescription drug). More information can be found on the Hillshire Accounts page.

    Will the health coverage rates be higher than what I am paying right now?

    In most cases, rates for health coverage will be lower in 2017! It is also worth noting that the cost of the Tyson Foods health plan includes core dental and vision coverage! You can find your health plan rates by viewing your enrollment guide, or by participating in an enrollment session.

    When and how will I be able to learn more about all of the benefits?

    Details are available throughout this site and in your enrollment materials (including your enrollment guide). During Open Enrollment, you can speak with an enrollment counselor or participate in an enrollment session online for further information.
