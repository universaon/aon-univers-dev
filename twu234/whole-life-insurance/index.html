Whole Life Insurance

  • What is Whole Life Insurance?

    • Could your family maintain their lifestyle without you? Whole Life Insurance helps to ensure that they can.

    Whole Life Insurance is designed to provide a death benefit to your beneficiaries if you pass away, but it can also build cash value that you can utilize while you are still alive. Find peace of mind knowing your family will be taken care of.

    Plan Features:

    • You can purchase coverage for yourself, your spouse and/ or your children.
    • Up to $100,000 of coverage is available for members, up to $30,000 for spouses and $10,000 for children.
    • Long Term Care assistance is included.
    • Whole Life Insurance is voluntary, which means you can purchase the precise amount of coverage that is right for your needs.
    • No physical exams are required to apply for coverage.
    • As the policy builds cash value, you can eventually use it to make premium payments or to pay urgent expenses while you are still alive.
    • Coverage is portable — you can take your policy with you if you change jobs or retire.
