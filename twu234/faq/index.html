FAQ

Q: Will my Allstate benefits continue to be payroll deducted?

A: Yes.

Q: How do I learn more about these benefits?

A: A counselor will be available at your location to answer any questions you may have, and enroll you in the coverage you choose. Please be on the lookout for scheduling information. If you have questions in the meantime, please visit the Service Numbers page here.

Q: What is guaranteed issue?

A: If a policy is offered with guaranteed issue, it means that medical questions are not required when enrolling up to an specific benefit amount. For some policies, guaranteed issue is only available if it’s your first time being offered the coverage. Be sure to take advantage of your first enrollment opportunity!

Q: Who can be covered on these plans?

A: You and your eligible dependents can be covered on these plans. Examples of eligible dependents are your spouse and children under age 26. Your counselor will provide more information on dependent eligibility.

Q: How do I pay for my coverage?

A: The cost of these benefits are conveniently deducted from your paycheck! There’s no need to pay separate bills, or send premiums to each carrier individually.

Q: What do I do if I need to file a claim?

A: If a claim needs to be filed, you will have to contact the plan carrier directly. You can start by downloading the appropriate claim forms here.
