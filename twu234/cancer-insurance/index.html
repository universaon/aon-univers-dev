Cancer Insurance

Unfortunately, itâ€™s very likely that you or someone you are close to has been affected by cancer. A cancer diagnosis can be overwhelming for both your mental health and your finances. Cancer insurance helps to relieve some of that stress. To help with the costs of cancer treatment that might not be covered by your medical plan, cancer insurance pays medical benefits directly to you.

The plan pays benefits for a variety of cancer-related expenses, including:

  • Internal cancer diagnosis
  • Radiation therapy and chemotherapy
  • Hospital confinement
  • Surgery

Plan Features:

  • No physical exams or blood tests are required to apply for coverage.
  • You can elect to cover yourself, your spouse and/or your children
  • Benefits are paid directly to you, in addition to other insurance you may have.
  • A Cancer Wellness Screening Benefit is included and pays an annual benefit if a covered health screening test is performed. Some covered screening tests include: Mammogram, colonoscopy, and pap smear (lab and procedure).
The policy or its provisions may vary or be unavailable in some states. The policy has exclusions and limitations which may affect any benefits payable.
