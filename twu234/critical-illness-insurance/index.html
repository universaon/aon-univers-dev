Critical Illness Insurance

  • The Value of Critical Illness Insurance

    • Critical Illness Insurance can help with the treatment costs of covered critical illnesses, such as a heart attack or a stroke. More importantly, the plan helps you focus on recuperation instead of the distraction and stress over the costs of medical and personal bills.

    With Critical Illness Insurance, you receive cash benefits directly (unless otherwise assigned) — giving you the flexibility to help pay bills related to treatment or to help with every day living expenses.

    Covered Illnesses Include:

    • Heart Attack
    • Stroke
    • Major Organ Transplant
    • End-Stage Renal (Kidney) Failure
    • Invasive Cancer
    • Carcinoma in Situ

    Plan Features:

    • Up to $30,000 of coverage is guaranteed for members who are newly eligible.
    • Up to $15,000 of coverage is guaranteed for spouses who are newly eligible, and $5,000 of coverage is guaranteed for children who are newly eligible.
    • A Critical Illness Assessment is included for you and your spouse, which pays an annual benefit if a covered health screening test is performed. Some covered health screening tests include: stress test, colonoscopy, or mammography.
    • Advocate and support services are also included. These added benefits help to guide you through tough times that may come while experiencing a critical illness.

    Speak with a benefits counselor for help calculating the cost of the benefit, which will vary depending upon your age, tobacco use, and the amount of coverage you elect.

    The policy/certificate of coverage or its provisions, as well as covered illnesses, may vary or be unavailable in some states. The policy/certificate of coverage has exclusions and limitations which may affect any benefits payable.
