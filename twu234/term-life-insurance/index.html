Term Life

  • What is Term Life Insurance?

    • Purchasing term life insurance is an affordable way to protect your family’s lifestyle. This uncomplicated insurance provides protection for a set amount of time- giving you the flexibility to purchase coverage for when you need it most. This coverage can help to replace your income in your absence, cover final expenses, and assist with paying off debt.

    Plan Features:

    • No medical questions or physical exams are required during your first enrollment!
    • You can purchase coverage for yourself, your spouse and/or your children.
    • Up to $100,000 of coverage is guaranteed for newly eligible members, up to $30,000 is guaranteed for newly eligible spouses, and $10,000 is guaranteed for eligible children.
    • After a set amount of time, coverage is portable — you can take your policy with you if you change jobs or retire.
    The policy or its provisions may vary or be unavailable in some states. The policy has exclusions and limitations which may affect any benefits payable.
