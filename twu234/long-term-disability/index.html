Long Term Disability

  • How Can Disability Insurance Help?

    • Protect your finances! Long term disability will help you recover a portion of your salary if you find yourself disabled and out of work for more than 180 days.

    If you also elect short term disability coverage, the long term benefit period will begin after the short term benefit ends- ensuring that you will have protected your paycheck for an extended period of time.



    Plan Features:

    • You can elect to recover 40%, 50%, or 60% of your monthly pay, to a maximum of $6,000 per month (for up to 24 months). The minimum monthly benefit amount is $300.
    • The benefit will begin after you are out of work for 180 days due to a covered off-the-job injury or illness,* and can last for up to two years.
    • If you are newly eligible, you may be able to enroll up to a certain amount without answering medical questions.

    The policy or its provisions may vary or be unavailable in some states. If you live in a state that has statutory disability benefits, your benefits under this plan may be offset by any statutory disability benefits received. The policy has exclusions and limitations which may affect any benefits payable.

    *Pre-existing limitations will apply. A "Pre-existing Condition" is a sickness or physical condition for which the insured received medical advice or treatment within the assigned period prior to the Effective date of the certificate. Benefits will not be paid for that condition for 12-months following the effective date. A condition will no longer be considered pre-existing at the end of 12 consecutive months starting and ending after an insured's effective date.
