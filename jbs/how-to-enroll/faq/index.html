FAQ

Q: When can I enroll in benefits?

You may enroll from your date of hire up to 30 days past your effective date.

Q: How do I complete my enrollment?

Call the Enrollment Center at 1-888-561-1552 for English or 1-877-561-1552 for Spanish.
Monday, Closed
Tuesday – Friday, 7am – 5pm MT / 8am – 6pm CT

Q: Where can I find benefit and rate information?

Your HR representative will provide a benefits summary with plan information and rates for 2017.

Q: What information do I need when I speak with a counselor?

Please have the following information available for your enrollment session:

  1. • Your full name, date of birth, and Social Security number
  2. • Full names, dates of birth, and Social Security numbers of each dependent you wish to enroll
  3. • Full names, dates of birth, and Social Security numbers of your life insurance beneficiaries

Q: How do I add a dependent to my benefits?

If you wish to cover your dependents under the medical, dental, and/or vision plans, you will be required to submit documentation as proof of eligibility.

You must fax or email your documents to 610-537-2203 or JBSPPCDocs@univers.biz. Please wait 2 business days for processing and then call in to complete your enrollment.

Q: Can I make changes to my elections after I am done enrolling?

If you need to make a change after you’ve enrolled, you may do so by calling the Enrollment Center no later than 30 days past your effective date. After that time, you will not be able to change your benefits unless you experience a qualifying live event as defined by the IRS (examples: marriage, divorce, birth or adoption of a child, change in employment status).

Q: What is my deadline to enroll?

You must complete your enrollment within 30 days past your effective date. See your benefits summary for details.

Q: Where can I get my confirmation statement?

Your confirmation statement will be mailed to the home address you have on file with JBS or Pilgrim’s Pride. It will arrive 1-2 weeks after enrollment. Please see your HR representative to confirm or update the address on file.

Q: When will the benefits take effect?

Benefit effective dates vary by location and eligibility group. See your benefits summary for details.
