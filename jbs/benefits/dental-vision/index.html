Dental & Vision Insurance

Benefits may vary or be unavailable in some locations or eligibility groups.

Dental Insurance

JBS and Pilgrim’s Pride offer dental coverage to you and your dependents through Delta Dental. Plan options vary based on location and eligibility group. Please see your 2017 benefits summary and participate in an enrollment session to learn about your plan options.

Vision Insurance

JBS and Pilgrim’s Pride offer vision coverage to you and your dependents through Vision Service Plan (VSP). The plan provides benefits for exams, eyeglasses and contact lenses. Plan details vary based on location and eligibility group. Please see your 2017 benefits summary and participate in an enrollment session to learn about your options.
