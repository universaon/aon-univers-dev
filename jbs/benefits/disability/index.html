Disability Insurance

Benefits may vary or be unavailable in some locations or elgibility groups.


Short Term Disability Insurance

Short term disability insurance replaces a portion of your income if an injury or illness forces you out of work for an extended period of time. Benefit offerings vary by location and eligibility group. See your 2017 Benefits Summary or speak with a benefits counselor for details.


Long Term Disability Insurance

Long term disability insurance provides you with an ongoing source of income if you remain totally disabled and unable to work due to an illness or injury. Long term disability benefits are helpful if your disability extends beyond the benefit period covered by your short term disability benefits. Benefit offerings vary by location and eligibility group. See your 2017 Benefits Summary or speak with a benefits counselor for details.
