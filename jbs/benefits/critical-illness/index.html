Group Critical Illness Insurance

An affordable way to supplement to your health insurance

Health insurance isn’t always enough to cover all of the expenses associated with a sudden illness. Critical Illness Insurance works as a supplement to your medical coverage, paying benefits that can help offset out-of-pocket expenses like deductibles and coinsurance.

How it Works

Critical Illness Insurance helps provide financial protection in the event of a covered serious illness. When you experience a covered illness, the plan will pay a lump-sum benefit directly to you — not a doctor or hospital.

You can use this benefit any way you choose — to pay for deductibles and coinsurance, cover expenses your family incurs to be by your side, or simply to replace lost earnings from being out of work.

What is covered?


Extra benefits just for taking a health screening test!

The policy includes a Wellness Benefit, which pays you an annual benefit for taking a covered health screening test, such as a blood test, mammogram, colonoscopy, etc. Ask your counselor for details
Covered conditions:
  • Heart attack
  • Blindness
  • Major organ failure
  • End-stage renal (kidney) failure
  • Occupational HIV
  • Coronary artery bypass surgery (pays 25% of lump-sum benefit)
  • Benign brain tumor

Covered conditions with time limitations:
  • Stroke (evidence of persistent neurological deficits confirmed at least 30 days after the event)
  • Coma (resulting from severe injury lasting 14 consecutive days or more)
  • Permanent paralysis (complete and permanent loss of the use of two or more limbs for a continuous 90 days as a result of a covered accident)

Automatically included in your plan:
  • Cancer
  • Carcinoma in situ* (pays 25% of the lump-sum benefit)

Please refer to the policy for complete details about these covered conditions.

Plan Features

  • Coverage is guaranteed issue if you enroll when you are first eligible.
  • Coverage is available for your spouse and children.
  • Premiums are paid through convenient payroll deductions.
  • You can take your policy with you if you change jobs or retire.


Rates

Rates vary depending upon your age, tobacco use and the amount of coverage you elect. Your benefits counselor will provide plan details and rates during your enrollment session.

The policy or its provisions may vary or be unavailable in some states. The policy has exclusions and limitations which may affect any benefits payable. See the actual policy for specific provisions or details of availability.
