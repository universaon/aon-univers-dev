Benefits

Medical & Prescription

JBS and Pilgrim’s Pride offer medical insurance through AmeriBen and Anthem. The plan includes prescription drug coverage through CVS Caremark at no additional cost.

Dental

Healthy teeth are an important part of your overall wellbeing. JBS and Pilgrim’s Pride offer dental benefits through Delta Dental.

Vision

JBS and Pilgrim’s Pride offer the VSP vision plan to help keep your eyes healthy and your vision sharp.

Read More Here!

Critical Illness

Critical Illness Insurance pays a lump-sum benefit directly to you if you are diagnosed with a covered serious illness like a heart attack or cancer. This benefit can help you cover out-of-pocket medical expenses so you can focus on recovery.

Read More Here!

Accident

Accident Insurance pays a lump-sum benefit directly to you if you are injured in a covered accident and need treatment. You can use the money to cover out-of-pocket medical bills and other accident-related expenses.

Read More Here!

Hospital Indemnity

Hospital Indemnity Insurance pays a lump-sum benefit directly to you if you are admitted to the hospital due to a covered accident or sickness. This benefit can help offset expenses that the medical plan doesn’t cover.

Read More Here!

Term Life & AD&D

Term Life Insurance pays a benefit to your beneficiaries in the event of your death. Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance pays a benefit to you if you suffer a covered dismemberment like loss of vision or a limb.

Read More Here!

Whole Life

Whole Life Insurance is different from Term Life in that it offers permanent protection. It doesn’t expire as long as you pay the premiums, which means your rates will not increase as you get older like they do with Term Life.

Read More Here!

Disability

Disability Insurance provides a source of income if you are ill or injured and unable to work for an extended period of time.

Read More Here!

Identity Theft Protection

JBS and Pilgrim’s Pride offer identity theft protection, credit monitoring, and more through InfoArmor.

Read More Here!

Flexible Spending Accounts

Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs) enable you to set aside money for eligible out-of-pocket health care or dependent care expenses and help you reduce your income taxes at the same time.

Read More Here!
