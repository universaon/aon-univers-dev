How to Enroll


Before accessing the online enrollment system, carefully review the information contained in your benefits guide before making a selection. Dependents may not be added to this enrollment system. You must provide documentation (birth certificates/marriage licenses) for all dependents to your local Human Resources contact. Once dependents are approved and added to DayForce, they will be in this enrollment system within 7 days. Your 2017 Benefits Guide is located under the “Benefits” section.


Two Ways to Enroll


1. Online

Once you have clicked on “Enroll Here” on the home page, follow the prompts to register and create your personal login credentials. Please provide the following information in order to confirm your identity:

  • Last 4 of SSN

  • Employee ID

  • DOB (mm/dd/yyyy)


When you have entered the above information, click “Look Up” to confirm your identity. Once confirmed, continue to follow the prompts to create your account, login and enroll in your benefits.


2. Call and Speak with a Benefits Counselor.

You can call the Enrollment Call Center and speak with a Benefits Counselor who will answer your benefits questions and enroll you over the phone. Counselors may not add dependents to coverage unless they are first added by your local Human Resources contact.
