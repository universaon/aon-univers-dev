Your health is important wherever you go. Many employees spend most of their day sitting at a desk. Others find themselves grabbing quick meals with low nutritional value or high in fats. This is why implementing health programs inside the workplace has become vital to helping prevent disease and promoting a healthier lifestyle.

  • Biometric Screening

    A biometric screening is a short health exam that provides information about your risk for certain diseases and medical conditions. For employees on a BMC medical plan, biometric screenings are covered at 100% for preventive visits when you visit an in-network doctor. The results of your biometric screening are the first step to healthy living because it can help you understand where you can take action to improve your health.


  • Health Risk Assessments

    A Health Risk Assessment is a health questionnaire that provides an overall statement of an individual’s well being. When taken across a population, a health risk assessment can identify common risk factors, such as obesity, stress, diabetes, high blood pressure, and high cholesterol.


  • Smoking and Nicotine Cessation

    Quitting is the single most important step smokers can take to improve the length and quality of their lives. Smoking, however, is not the only product affecting your health. Nicotine products - including dip, e-cigarettes, snuff, etc. – also diminish length and quality of life. Because of this, BMC charges a surcharge for individuals who are nicotine users.
