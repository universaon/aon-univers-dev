Live Healthy Wellness Program

Controlling health care costs is a partnership between Ollie’s and its Associates. We need your help so that we can continue to provide quality, affordable benefits now and into the future. How do we help manage health care costs? By making healthy lifestyle choices. Ollie’s wants to help you do just that…and we’re happy to reward you for your efforts!

Associates enrolled in the medical coverage through Ollie’s are eligible to take advantage of the Live Healthy Wellness Program offered by Highmark Blue Shield and Ollie’s. The program is designed to help Associates make healthy choices and save money on their health care premium!


Three Steps to Save


1. Complete an Online Wellness Profile.

  • Your Wellness Profiles will give you a summary of your current health status and recommended areas for improvement.
  • Access your wellness profile at www.highmarkblueshield.com. Click on Rewards Program under Wellness Programs.

2. Receive a Annual Preventitive Exam.

  • When scheduling your appointment, be sure to mention the visit needs to be coded as your Preventive Exam.
  • Print out the Physician Laboratory Test Results (PDR) form and bring it with you to your exam, and have your doctor complete and submit the form by faxing it to the number shown on the form. This form can be accessed at https://portal.ichtools.com.
  • Only the lab tests on the PDR form are required. Please note, this form can only be printed one time from the above web site.

    • Complete the certification online at www.highmarkblueshield.com.
    • Certify that you are Tobacco Free; OR
    • Certify that you will enroll in and complete the “How to be Tobacco Free” program.
