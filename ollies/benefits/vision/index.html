Vision Benefits

Eyesight is a precious thing, and the Ollie’s vision plan allows you to take steps in keeping your eyes healthy and protected. The Ollie’s vision plan provides access to vision care through Highmark Blue Shield Optical. Plan highlights are outlined below.

Use any in-network provider you like, just simply present your Highmark Blue Shield vision card. If you choose to visit a non-participating provider, you will need to file a claim for reimbursement, and you will be subject to certain benefit limitations.
