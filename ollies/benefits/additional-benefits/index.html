Additional Benefits


401(k)

Ollie’s offers a 401(k) program that will help you save money and provide you with more income for your retirement. It’s simple to contribute to the plan through payroll deductions. The money you put into the program is pre-tax and you don’t have to pay taxes on the money until you begin to withdraw it in retirement. The amount you put into the program is your money, and you will be able to take it with you if you leave Ollie’s.

Ollie’s also help you save by contributing a 25% match on the first 6% of your income that you contribute to the 401(k) plan.

You can participate in the 401(k) plan after 90 days of continuous employment with Ollie’s. Entry dates are the 1st of the quarter following your 90 days of continuous employment.

For more information, please contact Fidelity at (800) 294-4015 or visit www.netbenefits.com .

Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Ollie’s is committed to your health and well-being. That is why we offer all Associates and their family members access to the EAP Advantage to help you cope with personal problems affecting you at work or at home. The EAP provides a confidential resource for assistance with a wide variety of work/life concerns, including:

  • Everyday needs and life events
  • Emotional issues
  • Family relationships
  • Sleeping dificulties
  • Eating disorders
  • Smoking cessation
  • Weight control
  • Relationship concerns
  • Coping with a serious illness
  • Loss of a loved on
  • Workplace concerns

    • You and your family have access 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The cost of the service is covered by Ollie’s; however, if you are referred to an outside resource, you will be responsible for their fee.


    To access EAP Advantage services:

    • Call (800) 223-2271, or
    • Log onto www.harrisrothenberg.net (Username: eap, Password: advantage)
