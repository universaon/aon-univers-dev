Eligibility

To be eligible for coverage under our health, dental, vision and life insurance programs, you must work at least 30 hours each week. Coverage is also available for your legal spouse (subject to spousal carve out described below) and dependent children to the age of 26, regardless of student status.

Spousal Carve Out: If your spouse has medical insurance available to them through their employer, you will not be able to cover your spouse or add your spouse on the Ollie’s medical plan. If your spouse does not have other medical coverage available, you must complete the Ollie’s Spousal Carve Out Form and submit it with your enrollment forms to cover your spouse on your medical plan. This spousal carve out does not apply to the dental and vision plans, so you can still elect to cover your spouse on these benefits.
